### Why? 
I often find myself exploring a lot of new thing in life and on the internet. I really hate when it gets lost or I just end up forgetting about any of it. Thins is one way for me to document my thoughts and have some sort of a repository to point to and look back to.

### What?
This is my personal blog. Here you can expect everything. From reflections of philosophy, to the advent of technology to some cool-ass designs I've found. this is the space for me to chat to myself and document those chats in a structured way! If you want something fun and useful checkout [[Resources]]!

### When?
I will be uploading whenever I do something I find fun. Could be every day or it could be once a month. Is there a way to subscribe? nope.

### How?
Well this is the fun part! This blog is actually just a part of my notes app ([Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/)). I wanted a way to minimize the friction from making notes and jotting down my thoughts to publishing them on the internet. I have had a website and domain for the longest time. I never am able to find the time to update it and there is quite some friction from ideas to experiments to documentation to putting it up.

If you wanna try this out you should definitely try out [quartz.](https://quartz.jzhao.xyz/)
